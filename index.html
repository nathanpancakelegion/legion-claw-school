FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need coding experience? +
No. This course uses Claude Desktop and Cowork — tools designed for non-developers. If you can write an email, you can write an agent prompt. No terminal, no code, no APIs required for the core modules.
What tools do I need? +
A computer and a Claude Pro or Max subscription ($20–$100/month from Anthropic). That's it to get started. The course walks you through setting up Claude Desktop, Cowork mode, and MCP connectors for tools like Gmail, Google Calendar, and Slack.
How is this different from other AI courses? +
Most AI courses teach you to chat with a chatbot. This course teaches you to deploy autonomous agents that do real work without you sitting in front of a screen. It's built from actual business operations — not theory.
How long does it take to complete? +
You can deploy your first agent on Day 1 (Module 02). Most people work through the full course in 1–2 weeks at 1–2 hours per day. But you have lifetime access, so go at whatever pace works for you.
What if it's not for me? +
30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. If you go through the material and don't feel like you got your money's worth, email us and we'll refund you in full.
Will this work for my specific business? +
If your business involves research, writing, email, data, outreach, or admin — yes. The 5 core agents apply to virtually every knowledge-work business. The templates are designed to be customized for your specific workflows.
Is there ongoing support? +
Yes — the private Discord community is included for life. Ask questions, share your agent setups, get troubleshooting help. The course content is also updated as new tools and capabilities are released.
What exactly is an AI agent? +
An AI agent is an autonomous AI system that can take actions on your behalf — browsing the web, reading emails, writing content, updating documents — without you directing every step. Unlike a chatbot, it operates on the Perceive → Plan → Act → Reflect loop, completing multi-step tasks independently.
