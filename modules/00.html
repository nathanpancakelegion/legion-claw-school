Welcome to Legion Claw School

You're about to embark on a journey that will transform how you work. This course teaches you how to build and deploy AI agents—autonomous systems that think, plan, and execute tasks on your behalf. Whether you're a solopreneur, freelancer, or small business owner, AI agents are the force multiplier you've been waiting for.

What You'll Learn

Over the next nine modules, we'll guide you from zero to deployed. You'll start with the mental model that makes agent-building intuitive, then move quickly to hands-on creation. By Module 02, you'll have built your first working agent. By the end of this course, you'll understand multi-agent workflows, scaling principles, and have a concrete launch plan for integrating agents into your business.

The Course Map

This module (00) sets the foundation and gives you the road ahead. Modules 01 teaches the conceptual framework. Modules 02–04 are your hands-on core—building, prompting, and tooling. Modules 05–07 scale your thinking to advanced patterns. Module 08 is your launch playbook. Each module builds on the last, but you can revisit earlier ones anytime.

How to Get the Most from This Course

This is a self-paced course. We recommend setting aside 1–2 hours per module. Follow the exercises, take notes, and most importantly: build as you learn. The agents you create in this course can become the foundation of your AI workforce. Let's get started.
