The RICO Framework

Prompt engineering for agents is different from writing chat prompts. You're not just asking a question芒聙聰you're defining an autonomous system that needs to make decisions, handle edge cases, and execute reliably. We introduce RICO: Role, Input, Constraints, and Output. This framework ensures your agents understand their identity, receive the right data, follow your rules, and deliver results in a format you can use. A well-built RICO prompt can mean the difference between a tool that works 80% of the time and one that works 98%.
