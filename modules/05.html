Module 05: The 5 Agents Every Solopreneur Needs

Paid

The AI Org Chart

If you’re building a business alone, your time is your scarcest resource. The five agents in this module are your AI Org Chart — designed specifically to multiply your effectiveness across the core functions every solopreneur faces: research, content, operations, sales, and quality control. These aren’t generic agents — they’re focused on the exact workflows that will return the most value for independent builders, consultants, and service providers. By deploying these five, you’ll effectively have a team working for you 24/7.

Agent 1: Recon — Research & Intelligence

Recon continuously monitors your target market, researches prospects, and delivers structured intelligence briefs before every meeting. It reads your CRM, searches for companies matching your criteria, and surfaces hot prospects daily. Instead of spending 45 minutes prepping for each call, Recon delivers a complete brief in minutes. It learns from your sales conversations — which leads became customers, which didn’t — and improves its targeting over time. We’ll show you how to build Recon with web search, company databases, and your CRM data.

Agent 2: Ghost — Content & Communications

Ghost manages your written output — LinkedIn posts, outreach emails, proposals, and any content that needs to sound like you. This agent knows your voice, your pitch, and your values. It researches topics relevant to your market, generates outlines, drafts articles, and produces 1-2 pieces of content per week. Ghost doesn’t replace you for important conversations — it handles the repetitive writing so you can focus on closing deals and building relationships. We’ll integrate this with Gmail, your CRM, and your content workflow.

Agent 3: Ops — Operations & Admin

Ops automates your administrative work — processing meeting notes into action items, scheduling, task prioritization, expense tracking, and report generation. It keeps your business running smoothly by enforcing your systems and catching things that fall through the cracks. Over time, Ops becomes the backbone of your daily workflow, handling the unglamorous parts of running a business so you can focus on growth and strategy.

Agent 4: Closer — Sales & Outreach

Closer handles your sales pipeline — prospecting, qualification, cold outreach sequences, and follow-up cadences. It drafts personalized outreach, manages multi-touch sequences, and flags when a prospect is ready for a real conversation. Closer doesn’t replace the human relationship — it handles the volume so you can focus on the conversations that matter. We’ll integrate this with your CRM and email to create a seamless sales workflow.

Agent 5: Sentinel — Quality & Review

Sentinel reviews everything before it goes out — emails, content, proposals, reports. It checks for tone, accuracy, brand consistency, and errors. Think of Sentinel as your last line of defense. Every piece of work that leaves your desk passes through Sentinel first. This agent handles the quality control that most solopreneurs skip because they’re too busy. We’ll show you how to build Sentinel with clear review criteria and output standards.
